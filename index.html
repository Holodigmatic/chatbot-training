About This Guide

This comprehensive guide provides structured guidance for training and refining your AI-powered chatbot to effectively serve Medicaid members using your job portal. The guide is organized to show concepts relationally and implementation tasks sequentially, allowing you to understand both the "why" and "how" of chatbot optimization.

Your company's mission of providing meaningful employment for everyone, with a focus on empathy and inclusion for marginalized and underserved populations, requires a chatbot that can effectively communicate with and support Medicaid members who may face various barriers to employment.

This guide will help you leverage the admin portal and training interface to create a chatbot experience that is accessible (5th-grade reading level), encouraging, and effective at guiding users through your portal's features.
